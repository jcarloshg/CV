# Job Description - Fullstack (React, Python)

## Responsibilities:
- Design and implement interactive interfaces with a strong product and business focus
- Redesign the commercial CRM and communication flows with end customers
- Apply UI/UX techniques and AI-assisted design (prompt engineering, Claude Code)
- Ensure consistency, accessibility and high performance in the user experience
- Develop reusable components using React or Angular and JavaScript
- Integrate front-end services with back-end APIs and workflows
- Contribute to technical architecture decisions and development best practices
- Take an active role in roadmap discussions, prioritization and rapid iterations
- Promote a culture of continuous improvement and strong end-user focus

## Requirements:
- 6+ years of experience in Front End development
- Strong proficiency in JavaScript and React or Angular
- Experience in UI/UX, product design and building scalable components
- Experience integrating APIs and working with distributed architectures
- Proactivity, attention to detail and strong business orientation

## Plus:
- Experience with LLMs, Claude Code, or AI technologies applied to software development
- Ability to work in Spanish and collaborate with distributed teams remotely

## Core Competencies:
- Strong sense of ownership and accountability for outcomes
- Bias for action and ability to move fast with incomplete information
- Focus on measurable impact and data-driven decision-making
- Collaborative mindset, putting team and business success first
- Curiosity and adaptability in a changing, high-growth environment
- Background integrating AI into your workflows
