# Job Description - Full Stack Developer at RSUNION

## Responsibilities

- Full Stack Development: Creation of web pages, SaaS-type software, web applications, and mobile applications.
- Optimization with AI: Creation and improvement of AI-based tools for process and workflow optimization.
- Efficient Development: Regular use of AI tools to support the development cycle.
- Autonomy: Collaboration with the team and autonomous project execution.
- Proactivity: Proposal of technical solutions and constant improvements.

## Requirements

- Full Stack Profile: Solid foundation in JavaScript / TypeScript (Next.js and NestJS).
- Experience with AI: Use of AI applied to development (beyond just copying code).
- English: Good level to hold meetings and fluid communication.
- Mindset: Proactive, collaborative profile with continuous learning.

## Why RSUNION?

You will build real products, with technical freedom, using real AI, with direct impact on projects that are launched and used, and with real growth opportunities.
